MODY UNIVERSITY OF SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY

Mody University of Science & Technology is a private women's university located in Laxmangarh in the state of Rajasthan, India. It was established in 1998 by Mr. R. P. Mody, an industrialist and philanthropist.

It is providing various undergraduate, postgraduate, research programs in various fields like Engineering, Science & Humanities, Management, etc.
